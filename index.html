How I work

My Design Process

Step 01

🔍

Discover

User research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis to understand the real problem space before anything is designed.

Step 02

🎯

Define

Synthesising research into clear insights, personas, and problem statements that guide every design decision going forward.

Step 03

✏️

Design

Wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity visuals — iterating rapidly from rough sketches to polished, production-ready interfaces.

Step 04

🧪

Test & Refine

Usability testing with real users, A/B testing, and iterating based on data to ensure the solution actually solves the problem.

Who I am

About Me


I'm Mustafa Saffa, a UX designer and visual communicator based in Sweden. My work sits at the intersection of human-centred design, brand identity, and digital craft — with a belief that good design should feel effortless.

I approach every project by asking why before how — digging into user needs, business goals, and context before a single pixel is pushed.

Currently open to freelance collaborations, UX projects, and creative partnerships.
